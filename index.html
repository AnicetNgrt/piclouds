Nuages de Pi

A cosy storybook sky where each digit of pi sends another soft cloud drifting across the page.

Chapter One

The sky writes pi in pairs of clouds. Larger digits fly faster and settle farther from the center line.

  • The first digits are fixed.
  • Later digits continue with random values.
  • The pace follows the upcoming digit.
